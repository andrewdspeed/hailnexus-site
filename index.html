What We Do

Complete Hail & Dent Repair Solutions

From a single ding to full storm damage — we handle everything from first call to final inspection.

🔨

Paintless Dent Repair

Our certified technicians use precision tools to massage dents out from behind the panel — no fillers, no repainting. Factory finish stays 100% intact and your car's resale value stays protected.

📋

Insurance Claim Handling

We work directly with all major carriers — State Farm, GEICO, Allstate, Progressive, and more. We handle the paperwork, supplements, and negotiations so you don't have to.

🚗

Rental Car Coordination

Need a car while yours is being repaired? We coordinate your rental directly through your insurance claim so you're never left without transportation.

🛡️

Deductible Coverage

Ask about our deductible assistance programs. Many of our customers pay $0 out of pocket — your insurance covers the repair and we handle the rest.*

📍

Mobile Service

We come to you — home, office, or dealership lot. Our mobile units serve all three of our regional markets, making the process as convenient as possible.

🔍

Free Damage Assessment

Not sure if your vehicle qualifies? We provide a thorough, no-obligation inspection and honest estimate. If it's not worth claiming, we'll tell you upfront.
